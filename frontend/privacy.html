Privacy Notice — Student Achievement Tracker

Last updated: August 2026

This notice explains what information the Student Achievement Tracker (a.chandrashaker.in) collects, why, and how it's handled. It applies specifically to this app; the main site's Privacy Policy covers chandrashaker.in itself.

What we collect

When you submit a certificate through this app, we collect:

  • Your roll number
  • Your name (as it appears on the certificate)
  • Certificate type, position/rank, event or activity name, issuing body, and date
  • The certificate image itself (scanned or uploaded)

How it's processed

The certificate image is processed using OCR/AI extraction to pre-fill the details above. You review and correct every field before submitting — the AI extraction is a starting point, not a final record, since handwritten or stylised text can be misread.

Why we collect it

This information is used solely to maintain a record of student achievements for institutional purposes — reducing manual collection and verification work. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to achievement record-keeping.

Where it's stored

Submitted data and images are stored in Google Sheets and Google Drive, using the same personal Google account's standard access controls. We do not sell this data or share it with third parties outside the institutional purpose described above.

If you are under 18

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act treats anyone under 18 as a child, with additional protections around consent. If you are under 18, please make sure a parent, guardian, or your institution is aware that you are submitting this information before you proceed.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your submitted data at any time by contacting us through the main site's contact page. We aim to respond within a reasonable time and in line with the DPDP Act's data principal rights.

Changes to this notice

We may update this notice as the app evolves. The "last updated" date above will reflect the most recent revision.
